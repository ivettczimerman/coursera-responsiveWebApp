What is Office Maintenance?

This website provides a quality office maintenance tracker. It was developed in order to ensure the best working conditions and atmosphere for your office.



What to expect?

Activities completed in time, clear organization of the duties, more responsible staff.



How to use it?

Go to the Checklist page in order to see all the activities that need to be completed and their status. Each activity has its own page where you can check the responsible person for that activity, information about him/her and the description of the activity.
